# Ryan Law — GEO, LLMO, AEO… It's All Just SEO

- **Source:** Ahrefs Blog
- **URL:** https://ahrefs.com/blog/geo-is-just-seo/
- **Date:** 2025
- **Why saved:** Primary-source essay from Ahrefs content lead; useful for comparison-page / thought leadership angles in a B2B SaaS playbook.

## Summary

Ryan argues acronym inflation (GEO, AEO, LLMO) describes the same work as classic SEO: create relevant content on topics your brand should own, on-site and off-site. LLM visibility correlates with traditional SEO signals—freshness, brand mentions, YouTube, review sites.

## Playbook hooks

- Comparison pages: "Do you need a separate GEO agency?"  
- Email angle: "What Ahrefs' citation data actually shows"  
- Internal bar: don't sell clients a second stack—extend SEO workflow for AI answers
